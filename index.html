Clean Highlights

Get a plain text file of your Kindle highlights.

From your Kindle:

  1. Tap Notes from the Kindle menu
  2. Tap Export Notes
  3. Amazon will email you a CSV file of your notes
  4. Upload your CSV file over here →

From iOS/Android:

  1. Tap My Notebook from the Kindle for iOS menu
  2. Tap the share button
  3. Select Export Notes to Email
  4. Email yourself the HTML file of your notes
  5. Upload your HTML file over here →
